RnnL Dance Studio

We are RnnL Dance from NYC. Founded in 2016, our mission is to create a community with a positive vibe and bring creative art pieces to the global stage.We offer dance classes, produce dance videos, and create our own performances to build a diverse and fun dance community in NYC.

Our dance classes include JazzFunk, Hip Hop, Urban, Kpop, Cpop, Heels, Breaking and Contemporary and seasonal workshops are open to all levels. Whether you are new to the dance or an experienced dancer, RnnL Dance Studio is always home for dance lovers to show their passion, dedication, and creativity.

We are RnnL Dance. We're all IN LOVE WITH DANCE.
